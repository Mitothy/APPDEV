Masungi Georeserve
View Gallery

Masungi Georeserve is a 1,500-hectare conservation area located in the lush province of Rizal, just a short distance from Manila. It showcases a mesmerizing blend of breathtaking rock formations, lush forests, and diverse wildlife. The georeserve is renowned for its sustainable tourism practices, aiming to preserve and protect its delicate ecosystem while providing visitors with an unforgettable adventure.
